Our Facilities

Main Sports Hall

The Main Hall in the Mattie McDonagh Centre is a massive space that can accomodate any and all sporting, cultural and community events. It contains full-sized regulation futsal, volleyball and basketball courts as well as two smaller basketball courts with six basketball hoops in total. There are also four regulation badminton courts and three courts for handball. The top quality hardwood floor was refinished earlier this year with new lines painted for all the sports mentioned above. The local primary and secondary schools all use the hall for PE and the local clubs make up a packed schedule in the evenings and weekends of indoor training and competitions throughout the entire year. Since it opened its doors in 2001 the Centre has regularly hosted County, Provicial and National sporting events at every age level

The local schools and clubs as well as many community groups use the hall for hosting any number of events including drama productions fundraisers, awards ceremonies, concerts, craft shows and Enterprise Town events, meetings, bingo...!!! the list is endless. And last but most definitely not least, it is the main venue for the annual Ballygar Carnival, the oldest carnival in Ireland

Niall Walsh Community Field

The Niall Walsh Community Field is owned by the community for the community. Anyone and everyone can use the field for any sport they like. It's managed by the manager of the Mattie McDonagh Centre and is in comstant use by the clubs and schools, and sometimes by just a group of young people who want to meet up and 'poc around'. It is a beautiful setting, well-maintained, and is safely nestled in a quiet location away from local traffic. The design of the campus means that the Mattie McDonagh Centre is easily accessed directly from the pitch. The pitch is exclusively for under-18s and is an ideal size with portable and fixed goals all there to cater to every stage from 2-18 years (and even younger...?)

Dressing Rooms

The Home and Away Dressings Rooms are large and clean with plenty of seating, storage cabinets and wall hooks. They have en-suite walled toilets and hot showers. They are easily accessed from both the main hall and the Niall Walsh field

Fully-Equipped Gym

Another amazing space, the Fully-Equipped Gym has everything needed to accomodate sports teams, exercise classes and individual training including treadmills, rowing machines and full sets of both machine and free weights. There are gym memberships available to Mattie McDonagh Centre members or the gym can be rented by clubs and teams on an hourly basis

Kitchen

The Kitchen and large Meeting Room area are adjacent to the main hall in the Centre. The kitchen itself is of restaurant standard and can cater to anything from large functions to more intimate gatherings. The meeting room is large enough to be used for meetings, courses and exercise classes. There is a second meeting room on the first floor suitable for use as a classroom
